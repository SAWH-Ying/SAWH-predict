main_wt_par1.m is the program that processes the raw data into adsorption/desorption work data. It generates a series of intermediate data and stores them in a new folder, please make sure you have enough space on your disk.

main_wt_par2.m is the program that takes the results of the SAWH unit run calculations and visualizes them.

RH_cal.m is the necessary RH calculation subfunction

wt_evr_cal.m is the data processing sub-function
